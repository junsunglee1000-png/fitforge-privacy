Privacy Policy

FitForge - RPG Habit Tracker

Information We Collect

FitForge collects and stores the following data to provide our service:

  • Account information: User ID for identifying your account
  • Habit data: Your habits, completion status, and streaks
  • Game progress: Level, XP, boss progress, achievements, and spin wheel rewards

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud servers. This allows you to sync your progress across devices and ensures your data is backed up safely.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use advertising or ad tracking
  • We do not share your personal information with others
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

FitForge is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
